A party game for horrible people.

The game is simple: Each round, one player asks a question from a Black Card, and everyone else answers with their funniest White Card. The player who has played the Black Card then decides which answer is the funniest.

Public and private multiplayer for up to 9 players!

Entirely free and open source!
